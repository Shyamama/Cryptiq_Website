# CryptiQ

> CryptiQ is a post-quantum cryptographic migration platform. It finds the vulnerable encryption across your company and migrates it to quantum-safe standards, with your team approving every change.

The brand name is CryptiQ, also written Cryptiq, cryptiq, or Cryp-IQ. The website lives at cryp-iq.com.

CryptiQ helps enterprises inventory their cryptographic assets, assess quantum-era risk, and migrate to NIST-standardized post-quantum algorithms. Tagline: "Post-quantum security made easy."

Key capabilities:

- Cryptographic inventory: discovery and enumeration of vulnerable encryption (RSA, ECC) across your infrastructure
- CBOM and risk assessment: Cryptography Bill of Materials reporting with prioritized risk findings
- Post-quantum migration: transition to NIST-standardized algorithms (ML-KEM, ML-DSA, SLH-DSA), with team approval at every step
- Continuous vendor monitoring: in development

Why it matters: "harvest now, decrypt later" attacks are underway. Adversaries record encrypted traffic today to decrypt it once quantum computers can run Shor's algorithm against RSA-2048 and ECC.
